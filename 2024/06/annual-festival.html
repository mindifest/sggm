ALL GLORIES TO SHRI SHRI GURU AND GAURANGA. ALL GLORIES TO SHRI SHRI RADHA GOVINDAJI.

SRI GOVINDJI GAUDIYA MATH IS CELEBRATING the MOST Glorious and Auspicious Festival of the 11th Anniversary (Praktya Divas) of our temple opening and Deity installation on Sunday July 14th from 9 am to 1pm.

This is a very auspicious event for the whole family, so please PARTICIPATE and take the blessings from the Lordships.
